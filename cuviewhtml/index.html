Carleton University 3D Viewer
The Carleton University 3D Viewer is a viewer of 3D files(open sourced data format). It was originally developed at Carleton University, Dept. of Electronics for reading files from ATAR , the microfilm growth simulator. The main people involved in the development of the program are Cliff Dugal , Bryan Wan , and Tom Smy.
